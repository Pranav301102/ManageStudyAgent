# HireNodes - 3 Minute Demo Video Script

**Target Length:** 3 Minutes (~420 words)
**Tone:** Inspiring, Technical, Fast-Paced

---

### [0:00 - 0:30] Introduction & The Problem
**[Visual]** 
Upbeat, modern background music starts.
Screen recording of a typical, cluttered job board, cutting to a frustrated candidate staring at a blank text editor. 
Text on screen: "The Job Hunt is Broken."

**[Narrator]**
"The modern job hunt is fundamentally broken. We spend countless hours tailoring resumes to bypass ATS filters, only to freeze up during the technical screen because we aren't equipped to communicate complex system designs under pressure."

**[Visual]**
Quick zoom into the HireNodes pristine Dashboard. The "Start System" button is clicked. System health modules light up green.
Logo Reveal: **HireNodes**. Text: "The Autonomous Multimodal Career Proxy."

**[Narrator]**
"Enter HireNodes: a fully autonomous, multimodal career acceleration engine. It isn't just a scraper or an AI resume spinner. It's a complete, self-driving pipeline that finds jobs, aligns your profile, and rigorously trains you to pass the interview."

---

### [0:30 - 1:00] Autonomous Scouting & ATS Alignment
**[Visual]**
Transition to the Job Scout Hub. Show the visual graph of active scouts. Arrow points to "Autonomously Ingested: 139 Jobs".

**[Narrator]**
"It starts with our Autonomous Job Scout Hub. Powered by the Yutori API, HireNodes deploys persistent search scouts that monitor career pages 24/7. And they are intelligent: our lifecycle engine evaluates, optimizes, and prunes poor-performing scouts completely autonomously."

**[Visual]**
Cut to the Resume Optimizer. Split screen showing the original resume on the left, and the Pioneer Llama-3.1 rewritten version on the right. Highlight the GLiNER-2 entity extraction pill badges (e.g., Python, React).

**[Narrator]**
"When you find a role, our native Pioneer dual-logic loop kicks in. Pioneer’s GLiNER-2 extracts exact hard skills from the description, while Llama-3.1 rewrites your resume perfectly. We grade for both human readability and ATS entity-density, ensuring you get past the filters without keyword stuffing."

---

### [1:00 - 2:00] The Core: Multimodal Interviewing
**[Visual]**
Transition to the Interview Session. Emphasize the **always-visible voice panel** at the top. The user clicks "Start Recording". 

**[Narrator]**
"But getting the interview is only step one. HireNodes shines as a multimodal training ground. We keep the voice panel fully visible while you toggle between code and whiteboard workspaces—because real interviews don’t happen in hidden tabs."

**[Visual]**
Show a user speaking. The Modulate Voice Analytics sidebar lights up, showing live bars for Confidence, Clarity, Pace, and tracking filler words.

**[Narrator]**
"As you explain your architecture, the Modulate Velma-2 API processes your streaming audio in real-time. We don't just transcribe what you say; we measure vocal clarity, pacing, and confidence levels. If your voice wavers when explaining microservices, the system detects it."

**[Visual]**
User switches to the Whiteboard canvas. They draw a load balancer sending traffic to two servers. A "Reka Vision" insight pops up identifying the architecture.

**[Narrator]**
"Switch to the whiteboard, and the Reka Vision API actively 'sees' your diagrams, analyzing spatial layouts and suggesting architectural improvements on the fly while GLiNER-2 simultaneously extracts technical entities from your evolving code and speech."

---

### [2:00 - 2:30] The Feedback Loop & Study Plans
**[Visual]**
Cut to the Interview Replay screen. Show the transcript, the AI quality score out of 5, and the "Ideal Answer" generation.
Transition to the Study Plan page. Show the interactive weekly grid and the targeted study blocks populating.

**[Narrator]**
"After the session, review your full multimodal replay. Every stutter, every missed design component, and every resume gap is seamlessly fed into our Neo4j Aura knowledge graph. Gemini 2.5 Flash acts as our overarching orchestrator, taking those exact failure points and automatically generating a personalized, targeted daily study schedule with actionable mock sessions."

---

### [2:30 - 3:00] Conclusion & Vision
**[Visual]**
Fast-paced montage: Dragging a job card in the Kanban Application pipeline, the Skill Graph visualization, and the Github-style Activity Heat Map on the dashboard.

**[Narrator]**
"HireNodes manages everything—from the top-of-funnel job discovery to the grueling technical prep, all tracked in a seamless Kanban pipeline."

**[Visual]**
Fade back to the user, looking confident, acing a real video screen.
Fade to black with the HireNodes logo and web URL.

**[Narrator]**
"HireNodes. The best way to master a concept is to teach it to someone else. And now, you have a platform that truly listens and watches. Welcome to the future of career acceleration."
